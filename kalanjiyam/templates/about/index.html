About Kalanjiyam

Preserving Siddha Knowledge Systems through collaborative digital archiving.

{# MAIN CONTENT CARD #}

Kalanjiyam's mission is to preserve and make accessible the ancient Siddha Knowledge Systems tradition. Our platform focuses on collaborative proofing and digital preservation of Siddha texts, ensuring that the wisdom of the Siddhas continues to enlighten future generations.

Formal announcements about new features and releases are posted to our mailing list.

We are dedicated to the preservation of Siddha literature, including texts on medicine, alchemy, yoga, and spiritual practices. Our work is guided by the spirit of the Siddhas — combining practical knowledge with spiritual insight.

Our code is open source and available on GitHub, reflecting our commitment to transparency and community collaboration.

If you have questions, comments, or suggestions, contact us.
