Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data on Ambuda.

{# MAIN CARD #}

Ambuda is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it may be shared or retained.

Data we collect and how we use it

  • We record web access logs including IP address and visited URLs. This is used only to improve platform reliability.
  • Proofing content (uploads, transcriptions) may be published publicly on Ambuda.
  • If you create an account, we store registration data such as username and email for authentication and login functionality.
  • We use Plausible Analytics for privacy-friendly, anonymized usage statistics. See their data policy.
  • We use reCAPTCHA to prevent abuse. Data is handled under Google Privacy Policy.
  • Emails (password resets, confirmations) are sent via SendGrid/Twilio, processed under their privacy policy.
  • Contact form submissions are processed via Formspree: Privacy Policy.
    • {% set jsdelivr = "https://www.jsdelivr.com/terms/privacy-policy-jsdelivr-net" %}
  • Some frontend scripts are loaded via jsDelivr CDN: Privacy Policy.

Data use and retention

We do not share your data except where required for functionality or legal compliance. Public contributions may remain visible as part of the archive.

You may request deletion of your data at any time. Please contact us.
