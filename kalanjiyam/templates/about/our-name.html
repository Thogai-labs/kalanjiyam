Why "Kalanjiyam"?

The meaning and philosophy behind the name of our platform.

{# MAIN CONTENT CARD #}

When we first started planning this project, we knew we wanted to build a platform to transcribe and preserve Siddha texts. Since the platform would store texts in a digital format and make them accessible to everyone, we needed a name that reflected this purpose.

After much consideration, we chose the name "Kalanjiyam", which means "treasure of knowledge" or "repository of wisdom" in Tamil. This perfectly reflects our mission to preserve and share Siddha texts.

As a Tamil word, it is simple, elegant, and meaningful. It represents ancient wisdom systems that we aim to preserve and make accessible to the world.

The word itself embodies the idea of a complete and curated collection of knowledge — exactly what this platform is designed to become.

The name also reflects our commitment to preserving the cultural and linguistic heritage of the Siddha tradition, rooted in Tamil Nadu and deeply connected to medicine, alchemy, and spiritual knowledge systems.

We believe this name honors the tradition we serve while remaining accessible and memorable to a global community of contributors and readers.

{# FOOT NOTE #}
A name is not just identity — it is the philosophy of the system itself.
