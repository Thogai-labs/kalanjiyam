✅ Do include:

  • The exact text of the book — transcribe, don't edit
  • Hyphens and line breaks as they appear in the original

❌ Don't include:

  • Page numbers and page headers
  • Handwritten notes, stamps, watermarks, dirt
  • Extra word splitting or sandhi splitting
  • Transliterations of the original text
